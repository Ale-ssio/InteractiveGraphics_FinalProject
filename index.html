SCI-FI SHOOTER

MOVE the player with WASD

RUN with SHIFT

JUMP with E

SHOOT with SPACE

You have 10 BULLETS, no matter the gun

You can RELOAD in any moment with R

To BUY/CHANGE the gun, click on it with the crosshair

To KILL the enemy you need to SHOOT him OFF the plane

KILLING the enemy grants you 1 COIN

There are 3 CRATES

1 COIN: gives you a RANDOM COLOR for your bullets

2 COINS: INCREASE your BULLETS number by 1

5 COINS: gives you a RANDOM number of COINS [0-25] :P

In the TRAINING ZONE you can press the Yellow BUTTON to RESET the pyramid of grenades

CLICK anywhere to START PLAYING

PAUSE

Click anywhere to resume
